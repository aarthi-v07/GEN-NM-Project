Abstract

The **Educational Organization Using ServiceNow** project aims to digitalize and streamline academic and administrative operations within an institution. Built on the ServiceNow platform, the system unifies student, staff, and academic management processes in a single, efficient environment. It automates manual tasks, simplifies communication between departments, and supports data-driven decision-making. By promoting transparency, accuracy, and sustainability, the project helps create a smarter and more connected learning ecosystem.



Objectives

 To digitalize and centralize student and staff records.
 To automate academic activities such as attendance, timetables, and performance tracking.
 To improve coordination between departments through a shared digital platform.
 To ensure secure and accessible management of institutional data.
 To encourage eco-friendly and paperless administrative practices.

Project Modules

1. Student Management Stores and manages student profiles, attendance, and academic performance.
2. Staff Management: Maintains staff details, schedules, and workload assignments.
3. Course Management: Enables efficient creation, updating, and tracking of courses and syllabi.
4. Communication Portal: Provides a space for notices, announcements, and feedback exchange.

Conclusion

The **Educational Organization Using ServiceNow** project demonstrates how digital tools can revolutionize institutional management. By integrating various academic and administrative operations into one digital system, it reduces paperwork, enhances communication, and improves overall efficiency. This initiative supports a modern, data-driven approach to education, fostering a more organized and technology-enabled learning environment.
